The Skitarii Serpents

This fierce set of models was my first step into Kill Team. I wanted to make them stand out from the usual red color scheme for Adeptus Mechanicus. I really enjoyed cluttering their bases with scrap metal and gears to give them that scrap yard aesthetic.

The Spider Queen of Mirkwood

I was never a fan of the offical Spider Queen model from GW's line, so I gathered stl files to make my own. I love the corrupted look the crystal shards give to this model and really enjoyed the elevation added with the base. This is still one of my favorite evil models for MESBG.

Khandish Kings

These conversions were quite fun, and these two are the centerpieces of our Khand-Easterling armies. I really enjoyed the airbrush fade and decal work on their banners.
